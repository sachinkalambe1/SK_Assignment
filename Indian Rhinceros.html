INDIAN RHINOCEROS



The Indian rhinoceros lives primarly in northern india and Nepal.These
Massive beasts have some noticable physical difference from their
African relatives. Their segmented hide looks like a formidable coat of
natural body armour. It function a bit like one also, Flexible skin between
the thicker hide "plates" allow them to shift as the rhinoceros moves

Other Endangered Animals:
  • Whooping Crane
  • White Rhino
  • Sumatran Rhino
  • Toucan

Type Mammal
